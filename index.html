Style Guide

LOGO USAGE

The monogram is a representation of me, my brand and the primary visual touch point to the general public. Due to this great care must be taken when using this.

When using the monogram please refrain from scaling the logo in an unconstrained manner, it is important that the logo sits at a 30 degree angle as this is also part of the brand identity. When this logo is being used with other elements please allow at least 20 pixels of space. As a general rule the more space the better (within reason). This allows the monogram to hold a primary position when used.

USAGE GUIDELINES

When using this monogram a number of rules are applied. Do not:

  • Rotate or change the direction of the logo
  • Change the color of the logo (only colours used within the visual brand)
  • Use multiple colours within the logo
  • Add special effects to the logo
  • Use older versions of the monogram, previous logos, or any marks that may be confused with the brand
  • Resize the logo out of proportion

COLOUR SCHEME

The colour scheme of the brand is very important. The primary colours consist of Gold (#c09666) and Dark Purple (#15032d) with Grey (#bfbfbf) and White (#fefefe) being secondary support colours for toning and variety. This colour scheme should be applied to every element within the website and brand such as image borders always being in Gold.

TYPOGRAPHY

I primarily use Didact Gothic for the majority of text due to its thin, simple and geometric nature. It is easy to read when small and easy to parse when in paragraphs. Much like other elements of this visual brand it too is a small element that adds up to form this brands identity.

WORDMARK

The font used for the Word Mark is “Quicksand”. The wordmark must be displayed as shown; the forename and surname must be of the exact same width and can only be shown in this box formation. This allows room for negative space. I would prefer the wordmark to be used exclusively with the monogram however in certain exceptions, such as when displayed in a small format, the wordmark can be used by itself.
